Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Questionify.

How do I register or sign up for classes? +
Simply click the “Get Started” or “Register” button on the homepage, create an account, and choose your preferred subjects or plan.
Are the lessons completely online? +
Yes, all lessons, quizzes, and mock exams are 100% online. You can learn from anywhere using your phone, tablet, or computer.
What exams do you prepare students for? +
We offer structured lessons and mock exams for WAEC, NECO, BECE, and UTME. Our materials follow the official syllabuses.
What are the available subjects on the platform? +
We cover all core and elective subjects for each exam — including Mathematics, English, Biology, Literature, Government, Physics, Chemistry, and more.
Do you follow the WAEC/NECO/UTME syllabus? +
Yes, all our lessons and practice questions are carefully aligned with the official exam bodies’ syllabus for each subject.
Are past questions and mock exams included? +
Absolutely! You’ll get access to thousands of past questions, timed mock exams, and instant feedback with answers and explanations.
What are your pricing plans? +
We offer both free and paid plans. Paid users get full access to premium lessons, mock exams, progress tracking, and tutor support.
Do you offer free trial? +
Yes! New users can explore selected lessons and quizzes for free before choosing a paid plan.
Can I access on my phone? +
Yes — our platform is mobile-friendly. You can study anytime using a smartphone, tablet, or computer with internet access.
How can I track my performance? +
Your dashboard will show your scores, strengths, and weak areas for each subject, helping you improve with every session.
